******************
Transmitter Safety 
******************

.. warning::
   .. image:: img/HighVoltageWarningSymbol_BG_removed.png
      :align: left
      :scale: 7%
      
   The ZT100-B is a high power transmitter for geophysical purposes. It operates at lethal voltages. Only trained, qualified personnel should operate, maintain or service it.

* The ZT100-B ist used together with a power supply which is typically fed by a generator. Make sure to comply with all safety rules and recommendations that come with these devices.
* Depending on the country and industry the ZT100-B is used in safety rules such as grounding and bonding requirements of the whole system may vary. Make sure to alway comply with the relevant regulations for your survey.
* Before operating the ZT100-B make yourself familiar with all connections and with the operating instructions.
